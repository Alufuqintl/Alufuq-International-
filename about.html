About Us

Committed to Excellence in Global Food Trading.

Our Story

Founded in Dubai, the hub of international trade, Alufuq International has grown from a local distributor to a global powerhouse in the food trading industry. With a steadfast commitment to quality and service, we have built lasting relationships with producers and clients across the world.

Our journey is defined by our passion for sourcing the finest ingredients—from premium dairy and cheeses to gourmet pantry staples—and delivering them with efficiency and care.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred partner in the global food supply chain, recognized for our integrity, quality, and innovative solutions.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between premium global producers and the HORECA and retail markets by providing superior products, reliable logistics, and exceptional customer service.

Our Core Values

Quality Assurance

We rigorously vet every supplier and product to ensuring only the best reaches our clients.

Integrity

Transparency and honesty are the foundations of our business relationships.

Customer Focus

We tailor our services to meet the unique needs of hotels, restaurants, and retailers.
